About

Outside of university I do a bunch of different things. I recently oraganised a science festival in my home town, in rural Wales. I've also developed an exhibit for the art festival 'Light Up Lancaster', showing how sunsets on other planets come in a wide variety of colours. I have a deep love for movies, and enjoy seeing all of the incredible technology used to create unique effects.
